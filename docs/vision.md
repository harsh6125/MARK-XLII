# ScholarLink – Product Vision (MARK-XLII)

## Short Product Description
ScholarLink is a role-based platform that enables structured collaboration
between students and early-stage startups.
Startups can publish opportunities and review applicants through a clear,
purpose-driven workflow.
Students can discover relevant work, apply with structured profiles,
and communicate directly with startups.
The platform prioritizes clarity, simplicity, and structured collaboration
over generic listings.

---

## What is ScholarLink?
ScholarLink is a student–startup collaboration platform designed to
connect students with early-stage startups for structured work,
projects, and contribution-based collaboration.

The platform focuses on reducing friction in discovering,
evaluating, and initiating student–startup engagements.

---

## Target Users

### Students
- Individuals looking to work on:
  - Startup projects
  - Early-stage product development
  - Skill-aligned contributions
- Prefer clarity, ownership, and real work over generic listings.

### Startups
- Early-stage or growing startups
- Looking for:
  - Contributors
  - Interns or project-based collaborators
  - Short-term or long-term support
- Need a focused, low-noise way to engage students.

---

## Core Problem

- Startups struggle to:
  - Reach relevant students
  - Filter serious contributors
  - Manage applications efficiently

- Students struggle to:
  - Discover legitimate startup opportunities
  - Understand expectations before applying
  - Communicate directly after initial contact

Existing platforms are either:
- Too generic
- Too noisy
- Not designed for student–startup workflows

---

## Core Solution
ScholarLink provides a focused workflow built around:
- Clear role-based access (Student / Startup)
- Structured profiles
- Well-defined opportunity listings
- Direct application and review flow
- Controlled, purpose-driven communication

The platform prioritizes:
- Simplicity
- Transparency
- Structured collaboration
over growth-driven or social features.

---

## MVP Scope (Initial Build)

### Student Capabilities
- Account creation and authentication
- Profile with skills and interests
- Browse and filter startup opportunities
- Apply to opportunities
- Direct messaging with startups

### Startup Capabilities
- Account creation and authentication
- Startup profile
- Create and manage opportunity listings
- Review applicants
- Direct messaging with students

---

## Explicit Non-Goals (Out of Scope)
The following are intentionally excluded from the initial version:
- Payments or monetization
- AI-based matching
- Public social feeds
- Community discussions
- Mobile applications

These are excluded to keep the system
focused, maintainable, and purpose-driven.

---

## Product Philosophy
ScholarLink is built with the following principles:
- Solve a specific problem well
- Keep workflows explicit and understandable
- Prefer structure over abstraction
- Ship working systems before adding complexity

---

## Project Context
ScholarLink is developed as part of the **MARK-XLII** repository
and is treated as a long-term product build,
not a one-off demo or experimental prototype.
